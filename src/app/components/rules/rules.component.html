Structure

Group Stage
Max 3 players from a single team.
Here there would be 3 rounds covering the group stage.
Knockout Stage
Max 4 players from a single team.
Here there would be a single round corresponding to round of 16 fixtures.
Quarterfinal Stage
Max 5 players from a single team.
Here there would be a single round corresponding to quarter final fixtures.
Semifinal Stage
Max 6 players from a single team.
Here there would be a single round corresponding to the semi final fixtures.
Final Stage
Max 8 players from a single team.
Here there would be a single round corresponding to the final fixture.

Scoring

GAIN

Minutes played - 1 (30+) , 2 (60+)
Scoring a goal - 5
Making an assist - 3
Keeping a clean sheet - 4
(gk's and defenders only)
Saving a penalty - 3
GK atleast 3 pivotal saves - 2
Man of the match - 3

LOSS

Conceding a goal - 1, 2(3+)
(gk's and defenders only)
Receiving a card - 1(yellow), 3(red)
Missing a penalty - 3
Scoring an own goal - 4
Worst performing player - 2
ROLE MULTIPLIER
Captain 3
Vice Captain 2
Starting 1
Substitute 0.5
The squad is fixed till the knockout stage. You won't be allowed to make changes to your squad until a portal opens up for it.
*Squads will be reset after each stage. Decisions taken on performance bonus of players will be transparent and based on leading online rating schemes.
